Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra specifications:

Let’s dive into the all-new Galaxy S25 Ultra’s specs!
● Dimensions: Galaxy S25 Ultra’s dimensions are 162.8 x 77.6 x 8.2 (HxWxD, mm).
● Weight: The Galaxy S25 Ultra weighs 218g.
● Display: Measured diagonally, Galaxy S25 Ultra's screen size is 6.9” as a full rectangle.
● Camera: Galaxy S25 Ultra’s camera setup features 200 MP (F1.7) + 50 MP (F3.4) + 50 MP (F1.9) + 10 MP (F2.4) on the rear, while the front camera offers 12 MP (F2.2). It supports UHD 8K video recording at 7680 x 4320 resolution @ 30fps, slow motion at 240fps (FHD) and 120fps (UHD). The camera system also supports Nightography and AI zoom for enhanced performance.
● Durability: The Galaxy S25 Ultra is built with a titanium shell, has superior scratch resistance with a Corning® Gorilla® Glass Victus® 2 display, and has an IP68 water and dust resistance rating.
● Processor: Galaxy S25 Ultra’s processor is equipped with Snapdragon® 8 Elite chipset with CPU speed up to 4.47 GHz.
● Battery life: With a 5000mAh battery, Galaxy S25 Ultra’s battery life can support up to 31 hours of usage.
● Price: Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra’s price is ₱84,990 for 256GB storage, ₱93,990 for 512GB storage & ₱110,990 for 1TB storage.
● Colors: The Galaxy S25 Ultra colors include Titanium Black, Titanium Gray, Titanium Silverblue and Titanium Whitesilver and 3 online-exclusive colors - Titanium Jadegreen, Titanium Jetblack and Titanium Pinkgold.
● Galaxy AI: The Galaxy S25 Ultra is equipped with Galaxy AI features such as Circle to search, Chat Assist, Note Assist, Live Translate, and more.
